Spreadsheets

You are to download and modify several existing worksheets to make them a more useful product. To be specific, these worksheets are the 2013-2014 Annual Statistical Report of North Carolina Public Libraries, published by the North Carolina State Library. While all 80 public library systems returned their annual report form, not all items on each form were filled out. Data element fields that were left blank or were reported as N/A are generally entered as zero.
